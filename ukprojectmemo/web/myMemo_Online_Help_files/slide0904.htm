ONLINE HELP AND TRAINING
How do I view a report?
Click on the PDF icon to save a printable copy of the report.
TIP – How many reports you can see depends on your Label Group. RCA users can only see RCA reports while management and support teams can see all reports.
Click anywhere in the My Reports section on the welcome page to be taken to the report links below.
Click on the links to view the report as a web page.
TIP – To request a new report please email onestopshop@sonymusic.com with a brief description of the information from the Project Memo system that you would like displayed in a new report.
