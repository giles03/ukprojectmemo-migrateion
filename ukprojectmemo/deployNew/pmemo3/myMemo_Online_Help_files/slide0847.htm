How do I create a new project memo?  And the welcome page …
Click on this button to create a new project.
You will see your 10 most recent  projects listed here.  Support teams will see the 10 most recent projects across all labels.
You can search for existing projects here.
TIP - Searches can be by memo ref, artist, title, catalogue number, product manager, release date and submitter.
This area lists projects you are working on but are yet to be  submitted.  You should be aware no-one else can access any project memos in this area until you unlock or submit.
TIP – Use 1 project memo per project ie Dido single – all promos, mobile, physical and digital products to appear on 1 project memo.
TIP – Marketing can search for all project memos created in their own label group; the support teams can search by all label groups.
