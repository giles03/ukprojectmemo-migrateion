ONLINE HELP AND TRAINING
How do I fill in the project memo header? (2/2)
Choose  the Product Manager from the drop-down list (use the Provide Feedback link on the Welcome page to request a new Product Manager & use “tbc” until name is set up.
Choose your product type from the drop-down list.
Choose the local label from the drop-down list ie the label the act is being released on.
Confirm if product is local (UK signed) or international (signed outside the UK) – this information dictates behaviour of other system so must be correct.
 
Select Genre from the drop-down. This information is used  internally.
Select if Parental Advisory is required for stickering and online stores.
Select if artwork and music are being created in the UK.
Choose Local Genre from the drop-down list. This confirms which genre the product will be listed under online.
After all required fields are complete. Use this button to save your Memo.
