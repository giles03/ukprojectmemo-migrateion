What is a Project Memo?
§Q: Why do I need to create a Project Memo?
§A: The process of releasing a piece of product cannot begin without a project memo being submitted by Marketing. A project memo is used to apply for/confirm catalogue numbers and to start the process of setting up products on internal Sony systems. 
§Q: Who is it for?
§A: The project memo is used by Marketing to confirm formats; the GRAS department use the details on the project memo to apply for/set up catalogue numbers; the A&R Admin department use the information to create masters (where relevant); the Production department use the information to set- up and schedule physical products in RMS and to schedule all products in Glores. Digital Ops use the details to order digital products
§Q: Which departments are involved?
§A: Marketing, Production, GRAS, A&R Admin, Digital Ops, International Marketing, Arvato Customer Services, European Services, Finance
