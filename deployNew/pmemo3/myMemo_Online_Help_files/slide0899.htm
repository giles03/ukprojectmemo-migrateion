ONLINE HELP AND TRAINING
Dashboard Smiley’s explained
Happy: the monitoring point is complete
Worried: at least one monitoring point is overdue, or the Product number and/or Release Date are out of synch in Glores and Project Memo systems
Angry: indicates that at least one monitoring point is seriously overdue and needs urgent attention
Production Angel: used by Production department to update Artwork, Master, Quantity Sheet and Packaging Form monitoring points
Release Date Warning: the release date for this product has been set differently in Glores and Project Memo
On Track: the monitoring point has not yet happened, but it is not yet overdue
N/A
N/A: the monitoring point is not applicable to this product
