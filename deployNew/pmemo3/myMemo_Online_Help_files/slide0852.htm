ONLINE HELP AND TRAINING
How do I create promo formats?
This is the promo
Screen.
Enter your format from the drop-down list.
Enter the local catalogue number (generated by Marketing) if a UK signed act.
If an international promo, enter the catalogue number provided by the rep owner in Glores for this format.
This area shows links to the formats you have already created.
Choose your packaging spec from the dropdown list; if non-standard please add  details into “comments”.
Enter the date parts (artwork and master) are due using the calendar.
Enter the date stock is required at Derry Street.
TIP – the parts due date and delivery date should be 15 working days apart unless a conversation has been held with the Production dept to discuss alternative
Dates.
Enter the number of CDs/vinyl pieces being used in your promo.
FOC price line selected by default (free of charge ie not sold at retail).
TIP – the Promo screen does not include a field for Barcodes. The GRAS team should use the Comments box to add barcodes for Local Acts as these are required by A&R Admin.
