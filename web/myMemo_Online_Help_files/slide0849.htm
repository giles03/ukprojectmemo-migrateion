ONLINE HELP AND TRAINING
How do I fill in the project memo header? (1/2)
Select Repertoire Owner from the dropdown list (if an intl act, this refers to the signing label ie Columbia, Epic; if a UK signing check with Business Affairs.
Choose the contractual rights per contract held  with Business Affairs.
The Project Number is generated and issued by GRAS.
Choose your artist from the drop-down list using predictive text .
Enter the title using freetext.
TIP – All information to be spelt correctly as this detail will feed into other systems and to online retailers.
Select the UK label group ie area of business releasing the product.
TIP – On any page click this logo to go back to the welcome page.
On each screen, this area shows which screen you’re working in.
The GCLS Number field is completed by the Commercial Division only; the number comes from the US.
